Northwind Industries — Travel & Expense Policy 2026

Approval Limits
Expenses up to $500 may be approved by a team lead. Expenses between $501 and
$5,000 require manager approval. Any expense above $5,000 requires VP approval
and must be submitted at least 14 days in advance.

Reimbursement
Submit receipts within 30 days of the purchase date. Reimbursements are paid out
on the 15th of the month following approval. Expenses submitted after 60 days
will not be reimbursed.

Per Diem
The domestic per diem for meals and incidentals is $75 per day. The international
per diem is $110 per day. Hotel stays are reimbursed up to $250 per night
domestically and $320 per night internationally.

Mileage
Personal vehicle use is reimbursed at $0.67 per mile. Mileage claims require the
start and end addresses and the business purpose of the trip.

Home Office Stipend
Remote employees receive a one-time home-office equipment stipend of $1,200,
refreshed once every 2 years. Receipts must be submitted within 30 days.
