Northwind Industries — IT Help Desk FAQ

Q: How do I reach the IT help desk?
A: Email it-support@northwind.example or use the #it-help Slack channel. The help
desk responds within 4 business hours.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Visit reset.northwind.example and authenticate with your MFA device. Passwords
must be at least 14 characters and are rotated every 90 days.

Q: How do I connect to the VPN?
A: Install the GlobalConnect client and sign in with your company SSO. The VPN
gateway is vpn.northwind.example. MFA is required on every VPN login.

Q: What laptop am I issued?
A: Engineers receive a 16-inch MacBook Pro with 36 GB of RAM. Non-engineering
staff receive a 14-inch MacBook Air with 16 GB of RAM. Hardware is refreshed
every 3 years.

Q: How much cloud storage do I get?
A: Each employee has 100 GB of company Drive storage. Shared team drives start at
1 TB and can be expanded by request to IT.
