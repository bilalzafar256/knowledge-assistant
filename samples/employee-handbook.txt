Northwind Industries — Employee Handbook 2026

Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Core
collaboration hours, during which all employees must be reachable, are 10:00 AM
to 3:00 PM in their local time zone.

Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Working remotely for 4 or
more days per week requires written approval from a department director.

Paid Time Off
Full-time employees accrue 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Unused annual leave carry-over is capped at 5 days into the following year; any
balance above 5 days is forfeited on January 31. Sick leave is separate and is
granted at 10 days per year, which does not carry over.

Parental Leave
Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Secondary
caregivers receive 6 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Leave must be taken
within 12 months of the birth or adoption.

Probation
New employees serve a probationary period of 90 days, during which either party
may terminate the employment with 1 week of notice.
